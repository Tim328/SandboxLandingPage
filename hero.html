FAQ

Questions before you get started.

Everything you need to know about how Sandbox works.

Do I need to record the steps myself?

No. Sandbox automatically detects your pages, buttons and important customer paths and tests the user flow without requiring video recording.

How often does Sandbox test my website?

Sandbox runs its main checks every 15 minutes to detect critical problems as early as possible.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. Sandbox is designed for shop owners and agencies without requiring programming or testing knowledge.

What happens when Sandbox finds an error?

You receive a screenshot of the exact error, a short explanation and a practical hint that may help you fix the problem.

Can Sandbox restore my website automatically?

The planned rollback feature restores the last stable version when a critical problem is detected, without intentionally removing new customer data.

Which parts of my website are tested?

Sandbox focuses on checkout and payment flows, login and registration, forms, lead generation and other important customer journeys.

Does Sandbox work with WooCommerce?

WooCommerce is the initial focus. Support for additional platforms and shop systems can be added later.

Will real payments be created during testing?

The goal is to test checkout flows safely using dedicated test products, sandbox payment environments or configured discount methods.

Can agencies manage several websites?

Multi-site management is planned for agencies so several client websites can be monitored from one central dashboard.

What happens to my €79 if Sandbox is not launched?

If Sandbox cannot be completed and launched, the €79 Founding Spot payment will be refunded according to the applicable terms.
